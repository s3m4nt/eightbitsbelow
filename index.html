Jacque's 8-Bits Below

Jacque, a tireless 8-bit caretaker of the Seas, travels the world’s oceans ZAPPING plastic detritus - all the while avoiding the unsettling of our precious marine life.

Mission: Use the W-S-A-D keys to navigate Jacque and his Yellow Submarine through the ocean to pick up Random Plastic Junk (10 points each!) – but be careful to not disturb his Sea Friends (instant death!)

The game ends When 50 points is reached, Jacque's mission is accomplished!
